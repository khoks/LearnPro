## Description

Phase B of the product-grooming pass. Two docs:

1. `docs/product/COMPETITIVE.md` — explicit teardown of the platforms a serious learner has already considered and rejected (or stuck with reluctantly). Per competitor: tagline, what they do well, where they fall short, how LearnPro is meaningfully different. Grouped by category (problem grinders, guided paths, mentored, niche-focused, brand education, AI assistants, DIY general LLM).

2. `docs/product/DIFFERENTIATORS.md` — the wedge in one sentence; 7–8 specific differentiators with concrete examples; what we explicitly *don't* compete on; why now; how a competitor could catch up (the moat assessment).

The output of this story is the **north star** for Phase A (UX deep-dive) and Phase C (backlog expansion) — it locks "what makes LearnPro worth using" so we can prune feature ideas that don't reinforce that.

## Acceptance criteria

- [x] `docs/product/COMPETITIVE.md` covers at least 12 competitors across 7 categories, each with strengths/gaps/LearnPro-difference.
- [x] `docs/product/DIFFERENTIATORS.md` states the wedge in one sentence.
- [x] DIFFERENTIATORS lists 7+ specific differentiators with concrete examples (not abstractions).
- [x] DIFFERENTIATORS has an explicit "what we don't compete on" section.
- [x] DIFFERENTIATORS has a "why now" + "how a competitor could catch up" section.
